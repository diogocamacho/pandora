# Day 1

Leadership Journey

* Leading self (individuals):
	* Focus:
		* clarity on work @ hand
		* define priorities, timelines
		* deliver on results
		* assess resources that are available
	* Competencies
		* subject matter expertise
		* upgrade + execute at high caliber
		* willingness to engage others
		* know thyself
	* Key Stakeholders
		* direct reports
* Leading Others (groups):
	* Focus:
		* undeerstand goals
			* alignment
			* priorities
		* understand other perspectives
		* team dynamics
		* development
	* Competencies
		* decisiveness
		* listening
		* strengths + weaknesses of team
		* conflict mitigation / resolution
		* follow through
		* advocate for difficult/different goals
	* Key Stakeholders
		* Team / direct reports
* Leading Organizations:
	* Focus
		* strategy, people, execution
			* stragic thinking
			* communication
			* overall goals
	* Competencies
		* bi-directional communication
		* trustworthiness
		* humility
		* decision making
		* risk taking
		* technical competency
		* creativity
		* innovation
		* ability to delegate
		* time management
		* problem solving
	* Key Stakeholders
		* board + investors
		* company/employees
		* patients
