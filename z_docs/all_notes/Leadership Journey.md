### **Leadership Journey Overview**

Leadership develops across three nested levels — _self_, _others_, and _organization_ — each demanding different forms of awareness and leverage.

#### **1. Leading Self**

Focus is on executional clarity and personal mastery.

- **Core Tasks:** Define priorities, align on timelines, deliver results, and assess resources.
- **Competencies:** Deep expertise, self-awareness, proactive collaboration, and continual growth.
- **Stakeholders:** Primarily direct reports or individual collaborators.
- **Essence:** Discipline, accountability, and knowing one’s strengths and limits.

#### **2. Leading Others**

Shift from _doing_ to _enabling_.

- **Core Tasks:** Align team goals, understand perspectives, and manage dynamics.
- **Competencies:** Decisiveness, active listening, conflict resolution, advocacy for challenging goals, and consistent follow-through.
- **Stakeholders:** Teams and direct reports.
- **Essence:** Build trust, empower others, and balance empathy with rigor.

#### **3. Leading Organizations**

Move from _execution_ to _system-level orchestration_.

- **Core Tasks:** Drive strategy, cultivate people, and ensure operational execution.
- **Competencies:** Strategic communication, humility, trust-building, creativity, delegation, and decisive risk-taking.
- **Stakeholders:** Board, investors, employees, and patients (mission-oriented lens).
- **Essence:** Translate vision into structure and culture.


> “Leadership starts with leading yourself.”  
> The inner work precedes the outer work.

### **Seven Transformations of Leadership**

(from the “Leadership Journey” framework)

A developmental progression from self-centered to system-centered leadership:

1. **Opportunist** – Self-interest drives behavior.
2. **Diplomat** – Seeks harmony and belonging.
3. **Expert** – Values logic, skill, and correctness.
4. **Achiever** – Goal-oriented, performance-driven.
5. **Individualist** – Questions systems and norms.
6. **Strategist** – Integrates complexity, leads through vision.
7. **Alchemist** – Transforms systems and people through purpose.
