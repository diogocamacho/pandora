> [!tip] **Stoic quote for the day**
> _From the Daily Stoic. Think how to apply it throughout the day and life._
> >[!quote]
> >“Aren’t you ashamed to reserve for yourself only the remnants of your life and to dedicate to wisdom only that time that can’t be directed to business?” — Seneca, On the brevity of life
> 
> 

Today’s quote is an interesting one: do we even stop to think? Do we even stop to better ourselves? Why not? I often think about the time that I spend watching TV or playing video games. Yes, it’s fun, but couldn’t I dedicate, at least part of that time, to improve myself? Maybe read a few pages of a book or a paper, or think about the bigger picture. That’s the goal for today, starting that habit more purposefully.
