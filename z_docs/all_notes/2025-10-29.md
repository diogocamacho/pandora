> [!tip] **Stoic quote for the day**
> _From the Daily Stoic. Think how to apply it throughout the day and life._
> >[!quote]
> >“Each person acquires their own character, but their official roles are designated by chance. You should invite some to your table because they are deserving, others because they may come to deserve it.”  -- Seneca, Moral Letters, 47.15b
> 

This quote is all about #character. It's a reminder that you are shaped by what life throws your way, and that your experiences are what makes you. It doesn't matter how young or old, how experienced or not you are. Degrees are not a defining matter for character, as which school you went too or which job you held. This is why you need to look at individuals as individuals and their experience as a shaping their character. You may not _like_ their character, but you can be a judge of that later.
