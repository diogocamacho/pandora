> [!tip] **Stoic quote for the day**
> _From the Daily Stoic. Think how to apply it throughout the day and life._
> >[!quote]
> >"When philosophy is wielded with arrogance and stubbornly, it is the cause for the ruin of many. Let philosophy scrape off your own faults, rather than be a way to rail against the faults of others." -- _Seneca_

This is a statement about judging others. We shouldn't judge other by what they do or do not do. We can only judge ourselves. Let that be the lesson for the day: judge not, unless you want to be judged as well. Focus your attention inward: Our faults are in our control and we focus on fixing them ourselves.
