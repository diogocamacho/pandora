### **Interpretation for Circuitry Growth**

|Competitive Pressure|Strategic Implication|
|---|---|
|Mature RNAi incumbents (Alnylam → Silence)|They’ve normalized RNA regulation as safe & manufacturable; Circuitry should **borrow their regulatory playbooks** but **avoid composition overlap**.|
|Logic-branded mRNA cos (Strand, Senti)|They’ve primed investors for “programmable medicines.” Circuitry should position as **endogenous network repair** rather than synthetic gene addition.|
|AI-RNA toolmakers (Deep Genomics, Inceptive)|Their tools could accelerate your compiler; consider **licensing their design models** to shorten PoE.|
|Data-map cos (Immunai, Insitro)|Treat as **data partners** supplying perturbation matrices, not rivals; you own the _actuator_ space.|
|Delivery platforms (Arcturus etc.)|Necessitate early **FTO checks** and potential **co-development** to secure macrophage/fibroblast tropism.|
