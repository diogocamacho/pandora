**Scope:** AI+data strategy, data architecture, ML platforms, model governance, hiring, vendor/compute, delivery oversight, security/compliance, IP.

**Org Model: PBU-owned, PI-powered**
- **Reporting:** Diogo → Avak; dotted to Matt Davis for standards. **PBU roadmap guides application of standards.**
- **Decision Rights:**
	- **CAIO:** roadmap, architecture, hiring, within-line budget reallocation (±10%), vendor proposals.
	- **PI:** staffing mix and platform standards; approves deviations; staffs TL/bench.
- **Resourcing:**
	- **PI embedded scientist (Tech Lead)** + **contractor bench (2–3 FTE-equiv)** via SOWs tied to backlog.
	- **PBU core:** CAIO permanent; growth discussed at scale trigger.
- **Governance:**
	- **AI Steering Council (monthly):** Avak, CAIO (chair), PI lead, Ops/Finance, Security/Privacy. **Escalation SLA ≤72h**; default to PBU roadmap unless safety/compliance risk.
	- **Weekly alignment** (CAIO, PI TL, PM); **delivery standup** during active builds.
	- **Single intake backlog & prioritization** (triage ≤3 days; WSJF-lite).
	- **Change control (RFC-lite):** Standard / Normal / Emergency as defined above.
- **Production-grade DoD & Risk Tiers:** as defined above; **Tier H requires Steering sign-off.**

**Why this works**
- PBU owns **why/what**, PI owns **how**—without losing **executive presence** in CompBio/ML.
- Aligns to venture creation end-to-end; **embedded TL + bench** prevents single-point-of-failure.
- ProtoCos/NewCos start with **production-grade assets** (code, data contracts, monitoring, runbooks) instead of prototypes.
