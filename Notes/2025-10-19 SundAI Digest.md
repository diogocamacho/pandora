### 👨🏻‍💻 Prompt of the week 

This is a fun prompt to give you insights into your platform and your strategy. Replacing the computational function with a different function should give you an equal set of interesting ways to interact with a GPT:

You are a computational-biology/AI platform architect at a mid-stage biotech. Sketch the architecture (data, models, wet-lab loop) for a platform that accelerates biologic design (e.g., antibodies, multispecifics) with a 4-week design-to-lab-test turnaround. What are the key bottlenecks? Where do you inject AI/ML? What validation loops do you require? How do you measure ROI?
