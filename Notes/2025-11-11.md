> [!tip] **Stoic quote for the day**
> _From the Daily Stoic. Think how to apply it throughout the day and life._
> >[!quote] 
> "When you are distressed by an external thing, it's not the thing itself that troubles you, but only your judgement of it. And you can wipe this out at a moment's notice." -- _Marcus Aurelius_
> 

This is an interesting quote. It tells us that we should not be so worried about what other people think of us, or what we can or cannot do. Those are mental impediments, not actual impediments, and pushing through those and getting to your better self becomes the challenge. Don't let others tell you what you can and cannot do.
