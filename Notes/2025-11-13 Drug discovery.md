**Wojciech Dworakowski**

* Principles of pre-clinical development
	* DMPK
		* Assess how the drug is **A**bsorbed, **D**istributed, **M**etabolized, and **E**xcreted
			* how doest the drug moves through the body and informs human dosing
			* regardless of modality, the overarching principle is the same
			* Poor understanding of ADME may lead to PK drug-drug interactions
		* Exposure
			* complex therapeutics lead to a comprehensive analysis of different bioanalytic methods to deconstruct exposure 
			* tracking only drug bound is not sufficient -> we need the unbound concentration
			* exposure also drives safety evaluation
				* for us to truly understand risk, we need to understand risk (exposure) and not just if it’s hazardous (toxicology)
	* Toxicology
		* seeing tox in preclinical studies is NOT a failure and a necessary endpoint
		* if we DO NOT see tox, we need to ask why! 
	* Efficacy/Pharmacodynamics
	* Modeling and simulation
