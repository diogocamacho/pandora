**Innovation**

> Innovation is a **new solution** to an **important problem** that **generates value** #quote 
 

>  “Companies are being run by technologists. You need to create products that are a solution to society, and not focusing on your technology.” #quote 




**Serial Innovation**

* Only a small number of biotech companies have been able to figure out how to continuously invest in R&D while pushing forward in the sales of the existing products
* 2/3 of OpEcs @ Vertex are still used in R&D



**Tech an Bio**

> “work fast and break things”, from Silicon Valley, does **NOT** work in drug discovery #quote 




**Human Genetics**

* Use human genetics to find drugs
* different from “shots on goal”

> small number of high quality projects is better than a large number of low quality projects #quote
