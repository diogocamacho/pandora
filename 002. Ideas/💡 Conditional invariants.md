> [!tip] The core idea
> Imagine that a healthy transcriptional profile is a taut line. now imagine that any deviation from this line, in any direction, can lead to some disease phenotype. what if there was a way to identify which of the genes in that transcriptional profile do not change and yet contribute to driving that disease phenotype.


This is something that I have started to explore with [[@Vini Mani]]. The idea is kinda neat, as it moves us away from the standard "I'm going to look at the genes/proteins that change the most" idea that drives a lot of the discourse in target ID and disease biology and so on. I plugged this into BioArchitect to explore some ideas (yeah, yeah, it's not concept generator. big, fucking whoop.)
