> [!tip] The core idea
> This idea comes from Mike and Raffi, on how we can create Axcella 2.0. In essence, can we learn how Axcella did their product memos and build on that? 

This goes back to my conversation with Mike ([[2025-10-30 Mike <> Diogo]]) on how we can learn from the the memos that Axcella put together to build products. The idea would be to:

- Learn how the memos were put together and unpack that
- Build a system that takes from those learnings and creates novel products
- Push that on to Extuitive
- Get those products generated and shipped to market in record time.
