## 🧠 Thought/note  
### RNA as a therapeutic medium is finally domesticated.  
Chemistry (N1-methyl-pseudouridine, optimized cap analogs, ionizable lipids) and manufacturing pipelines have de-risked RNA delivery and stability. Post-Moderna and BioNTech, regulators and investors understand RNA as a reliable therapeutic substrate, opening the door to more complex constructs.

#### Control theory meets biology.  
Systems biology teams now routinely use controllability and network-flow metrics (from engineering) to predict how small sets of perturbations shift cell states. The conceptual gap between “electrical circuits” and “gene circuits” has narrowed to a usable engineering framework — which is the philosophical core of Circuitry Therapeutics.
