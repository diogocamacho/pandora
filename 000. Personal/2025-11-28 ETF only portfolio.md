# How to Allocate Each $5k Tranche

Every 6 weeks, deploy the $5k using this base recipe. If any bucket is overweight, direct that portion into the underweight one.

### **Per-Tranche Blueprint (~$5,000)**

**SPY – $1,000**  
Keeps your ballast at ~30 percent. If SPY is overweight, skip and redirect to IWM or VXUS.

**Tech ETF (QQQ/FTEC) – $1,000**  
Deepens your AI/tech exposure without single-name risk.

**SMH – $1,000–1,200**  
Your growth engine. If semis lag, push toward $1,200. If they run hot, drop to $800 and redirect to biotech.

**XBI – $700**  
High-beta biotech. Beneficial when markets rotate.

**ARKG – $600**  
Genomics + synthetic biology. More volatile but highly decorrelated with semis.

**IWM – $300**  
Small allocation but crucial to maintain factor diversity.

**VXUS – $200**  
Keeps you globally diversified without dragging returns much.

Total ~ $4,800–5,000 depending on SMH range.
