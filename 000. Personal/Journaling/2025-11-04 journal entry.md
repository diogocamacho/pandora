### 📓 Reflection
> [!question] What’s occupying your mind?

This was the learning for the day: “There is no evil in things changing, just as there is no good in persisting in a new state” [[2025-11-04]] #stoic. I read this as an invitation to be constantly evolving, constantly learning, constantly bettering oneself, constantly with a #growth mindset. This is something I attempt to do, even if sometimes I can get stuck in my ways. Ok, fine, maybe not sometimes. Maybe being stubborn prevents me from having a growth mindset. 

I am trying to be more forgiving at home, but I think I'm still failing. There's no amount of celexa or any other SSRI that would mellow me out. One of the things that I think is bizarre but a consequence of growing up and having different responsibilities is the fact that mellow me seems to be a memory of the past. I need to find a way to rescue that, so that I can be more present, more calm, more engaged. I will blame the phone (jokingly), but I do need to find a way of remaining in the moment. 

Maybe that's a goal for tomorrow, and going forward. Put the phone down, enjoy the moment. Especially with the kids.
